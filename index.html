About

Sound as material, performance as a form of listening.

Helena Tan Li is a percussionist and interdisciplinary artist focused on contemporary repertoire, collaborative creation, and performance that treats sound as a sculptural material.

Her work moves between concert music, sound installation, and intimate studies of resonance: metal, wood, skin, breath, electricity, and the room itself.
